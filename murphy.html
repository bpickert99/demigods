The Signature
Always True
Fire responds to you. Flames in your presence burn longer and brighter. Dying fires revive at your touch. You cannot be harmed by ordinary fire — Brigid's gift means the flame knows you as kin. Bres's fire is another matter entirely.
The Divine Instrument
Brigid's Demand
Once per Quest, Brigid asks something of you. She communicates through warmth that arrives in your hands without warning, through a healer's compulsion you cannot explain, through dreams of the sacred wells.
What she tends to want: Heal someone who cannot pay for it. Create something for someone who has nothing — a meal, a shelter, a working tool. Bear witness to suffering without looking away. Use your gift for someone your instinct tells you not to trust.
Comply: Mark your Divine Track. No roll.
Refuse: Mark a Refusal. At three, all divine moves that roll +Pull cost 1 Harm until reconciliation. Brigid reconciles through acts of genuine healing or creation — not apology.
