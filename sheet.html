Who Are You?
Give your character a name. This is how you'll be known in the world and on your sheet.
Choose Your Hero Playbook
Your playbook defines your heroic archetype and gives you a set of moves. You can browse the moves for each playbook before choosing. Your choice is permanent once confirmed.
Choose Your Birth God
Every hero is born under the influence of a divine patron. Your birth god grants you a set of divine moves to unlock over time, and their instrument defines your relationship to the divine world.
Assign Your Scores
Drag each value to the score it fits. Your array is +2, +1, +1, 0, −1. Scores describe how you approach the world — not just what you're good at.
Bold — direct action, force, combat
Sharp — perception, knowledge, medicine
Slick — deception, stealth, subtlety
Heart — empathy, persuasion, connection
Steel — endurance, restraint, grit

Your anchor is a mortal person or relationship that connects you to the human world. As you pull toward the divine, they become the reason you return.
