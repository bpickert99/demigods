The Signature
Always True
You understand how things are built. Any constructed object — weapon, machine, building, trap — yields its secrets to you on examination. You always know the fastest way to build or break any constructed thing. This is not a power. This is how you think.
The Divine Instrument ?
Goibniu's Demand ?
Once per Quest, the Morrigan asks something of you. The Weaver delivers this through crows, a dream, or direct visitation.
What he tends to want: Repair something important that no one else can fix. Build something for the group that will be genuinely useful. Teach someone a craft skill they will keep. Create a weapon or tool worthy of a significant challenge ahead.
Comply: Mark your Divine Track. No roll.
Refuse: Mark a Refusal. At three, all +Pull divine moves cost 1 Harm until reconciliation. Goibniu reconciles through acts of genuine craft — something built with care, not speed.
